📒

"What did I like about it?"

The solution

One photo. The whole story.

一

Snap & translate

Point your camera at the label. We read the kanji, the brewery, the grade — in your language.

二

Build your taste profile

Tap quick tags. Save tasting notes. Watch your preferences emerge — fruity, dry, floral, sharp.

三

Loved it? See where it ships globally. Trusted importers in your country, with prices and reviews.
